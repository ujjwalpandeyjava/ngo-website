Voice of Slum has been dedicated to serving slum children since its establishment in 2016.

Voice of Slum was established in 2016 by Chandni Di and Dev Pratap, who themselves grew up in a slum.

Having endured the struggles of slum life firsthand, they were determined to spare other children from similar hardships and provide them with the childhood every child deserves.
